My Travelogue.

Australia


Australia is one of the most wonderful places that I have visited and the varied types of places you see there cannot be seen anywhere else. Australia has beaches, cold cities and Theme parks as well. This is what makes Australia a family place. You can go there with your entire family and I guarantee you that you will enjoy.



Singapore


Singapore is one of the many places which I like because of it's varied culture and the kind of people who live there. I am an Indian and I did not feel as an outsider in Singapore becuase people in Singapore are all from different cultures and they live together as a community.


Thailand


Thailand is one of the countries where I would go if I want a holiday for chilling and enjoying! It has a lot of water activites and also the atmosphere is very cheerful there.
